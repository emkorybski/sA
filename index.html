About Us

Gentle Dog’s Club is a force-free, science based Dog Training Centre, which also provides top quality Dog Walking and Cage-free relaxing Dog Minding services in North Dublin, within easy reach of the Dublin City and the M50. We believe that every dog has a gentle side awaiting to be uncovered and we are here to support you in achieving your dream of having a well balanced dog you want to go on adventures with. We also like our dogs spoiled while in our care 🙂 – something that every furry gentleman and a lady needs.
